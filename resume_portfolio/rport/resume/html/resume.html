About Me

Hi, I'm Dom! I'm a current program developer at Amazon currently maintaining an internal network wide website dedicated to providing resources to equipment tracking and the storage of excel tools for operations, safety and etcetera (Don't be afraid to ask me about watchtower!).

I began my career as a data analyst sometime after my graduation from UC Riverside (B.S Physics), where I concentrated in the study of Semi-Conductors and of their applications in modern day electronics. After working closely with large amounts of data and trying to find insiteful meanings from them, I found that I was more inclined towards data analytics and full stack development with emphasis on data science. (I have a broad skillset with strong skills geared towards my passion,) I am a self taught python guru with experience and knowledge in Ruby on Rails, python Django, Java, and JS. Give me a goal and I'll diligently work to achieve it!

I'm currently working as a program developer at Amazon (also known as a full stack developer), working my way towards machine learning and artificial intelligence. I feel to effectively be a strong candidate, I must understand and master the basics to programming and displaying data. To showcase my experience, I first have to create a means to show it. That's where my full stack development experience comes in. Can you imagine the difficulty of displaying data without knowing how insertions work in HTML? Or pipelines for data analytics? By understanding and being able to work on all of this independently, my skills would reduce my need to rely on other individuals who could take hours to days finding a solution to. That's why I am working towards Data Science from a full stack developer's perspective. If you have any questions, feel free to always reach out via email.
